Lamb Burger with Onion Soup Aioli (20 min)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground lamb
  • 1 tablespoon onion soup mix
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese
  • 4 brioche buns, split and toasted
  • 1 cup arugula
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  • In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb with the onion soup mix. Form the mixture into four 4-inch patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and lemon juice to make the aioli. Set aside.
  • Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the lamb patties and cook until browned on the bottom, about 3 minutes. Flip the patties and cook until the desired doneness, about 3 minutes longer for medium. During the last minute of cooking, top each patty with a slice of Swiss cheese and cover the skillet to melt the cheese.
  • Spread the aioli on the cut sides of the toasted brioche buns. Place the arugula on the bottom buns, top with the lamb patties, then add the tomato slices. Cover with the top buns and serve immediately.

Make Ahead

The aioli can be made up to 1 day ahead and refrigerated. Reheat gently before serving.
