Garlic Fried Rice (35 min)

This Fried Rice that is fragrant with the aroma of Garlic is very easy to make and can be made very quickly. Here is a detailed Recipe with images and a video. Indo-Chinese recipes are very quick to make and they make for very good party dishes. This Burnt Garlic Fried Rice tastes great with Tomato Ketchup or Indo-Chinese Starters.

Tips:

  • Garlic: The Fried Rice tastes great if the chopped Garlic is fried well but do not let the Garlic burn as the Rice becomes bitter.
  • Basmati Rice: I have used Basmati Rice but you could use any other rice as well.

How to make Rice fluffy:

  • Soak Rice for 30 minutes. Add 4 cups of Water, Salt and Oil and cook to 80% on a high flame.
  • Cooking Rice to 80% means when you break a grain, it should still be a little solid. At that stage, strain the Rice and spread it on a plate coated with Oil or in a sieve and let it cool down fully. Then you get fluffy, non-sticky Rice.

Aromatic powder:

  • I have used Aromatic powder found in the market instead of Ajinomoto. This is a substitute for Ajinomoto. In case you like Ajinomoto, you could use it or go without it.
