Basic Pizza (30 min)

This pizza is a simple yet delicious dish that you can make quickly. It's perfect for any occasion and goes well with various toppings.

Ingredients of Pizza:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onions
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 100 gm processed cheese
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • water as required

Preparation:

  • Step 1: Prepare the pizza dough

    • Take a dough kneading plate and add all-purpose flour to it. Next, add salt and baking powder and sieve the flour once. Then, make a well in the center and add 1 teaspoon of oil. Take warm water, mix the yeast in it along with 1 teaspoon of sugar. Mix well and keep aside for 10-15 minutes until the yeast rises. Once risen, add it to the flour and knead the dough using some water. Keep the dough aside for 4-6 hours, then knead it again. Now, the pizza dough is ready.

  • Step 2: Prepare the pizza base

    • Preheat the oven to 180째C. Dust the surface with dry flour and take a large amount of pizza dough. Roll the dough into a circular base using a rolling pin. Use a fork to prick the base so it doesn't rise and bakes evenly. Place it in the preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes until the pizza base is ready.

  • Step 3: Chop all the vegetables for the pizza

    • Wash the capsicum and slice it thinly. Peel and thinly slice the onions. Cut the tomatoes and mushrooms in similar thin slices, ensuring the tomatoes have less juice. Grate the processed and mozzarella cheese separately.

  • Step 4: Spread the sauce and veggies on the base

    • Take the fresh pizza base and spread tomato ketchup all over it. Sprinkle half of the processed cheese evenly over the base, then distribute the vegetables across the base. Finish with a thick layer of mozzarella cheese.

  • Step 5: Bake the pizza at 250째C for 10 minutes

    • Place the pizza on a baking tray and bake in the oven at 250째C for 10 minutes. Once done, remove from the oven, sprinkle oregano and chili flakes according to taste, and serve hot.

Tips:

  • Make sure to preheat the oven adequately. For a standard oven, preheat to 280째C.
  • If using a ready-made pizza base, ensure it's not cold. Take it out half an hour before making the pizza.
  • Stretch the dough to make the base rather than rolling it, especially if making a fresh pizza base.
  • Using a pizza stone helps the dough rise and makes it crispy, ideal for a restaurant-style pizza.
  • For an organic touch, use fresh tomato puree or paste instead of ketchup for a more flavorful pizza.
